What is baseless?

Baseless is designed to help developers (and designers) in prototyping web apps and web sites.

Baseless provides most of the simple styling you will need to mock up a quick working prototype, and will help you quickly tweak the styles when you do have more time.

How is it different?

There are too many "grid systems" and bootstraps that don't actually help you get anything done. You spend all your time figuring out how to use them. With baseless just write markup your way and with the generic css rules, it won't look like an eyesore. If you want to float a column or make something fluid do it yourself! — some grid systems can hold you back and make a mess of your markup, most of the time you're be better off just doing this yourself.

Who/what it baseless for?

  • Web developers looking to prototype something fast, making time for lots of backend coding, without having to worry about the front end for a little while.
  • Web designers looking to mockup a very quick site structure and get content in place.
  • A template for people writing content centric websites such as blogs.
  • Whatever else you guys want to do with it!

How do I get it?
